e-Business

Abstract

This book attempts to introduce undergraduate students to the nature and requirements for conducting business online. It starts with a discussion of the nature of business and the challenges and potential of the online environment, followed by a review of common methods of modelling business, and a study of open source business solutions. The final chapter focuses on emerging trends and sea-changes in e-Business.
